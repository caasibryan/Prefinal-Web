Frequently Asked Questions

Q:Can I make it at home?
A: Yes! Brew coffee, steam milk, and add your favorite chocolate syrup or powder.

Q: Is there a non-caffeinated version?
A:Yes, you can make a decaf chocolate latte or a hot chocolate latte (without coffee).

Q: Can I use plant-based milk?
A: Yes! Almond, oat, or soy milk works great in a chocolate latte.

Q: What's the difference between a mocha and a chocolate latte?
A:A mocha is a chocolate latte with whipped cream, while a chocolate latte focuses more on the creamy texture and rich chocolate flavor.
